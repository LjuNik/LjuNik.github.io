What is Psychotherapy?

Psychotherapy is a type of counselling. Psychotherapy involves a treatment of a mental health disorder such OCD, GAD, BPD, Depression, while counselling also includes support of symptoms and development of coping skills to deal with life’s challenges. The two terms are used interchangeably, a psychotherapist is also a counsellor.

Services

I help individuals with mental and emotional wellness through the application of psychotherapeutic evidence-based treatment modalities as well as broader counselling techniques, body-oriented therapy, coaching, hypnosis, spiritual and energetic work, to treat mental health disorders and support the healing process of mind, body and soul.

Circle of Care

Mental wellness support does not begin or end with counselling. A counsellor is only one person who takes part in individual’s healing process. A strong support networks of trusted healthcare professionals is a must to a holistic health. Build your circle of care with these trusted mental heath oriented care providers to support your healing journey on mind, body and soul level.
